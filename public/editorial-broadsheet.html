Roasted Chicken Thighs With Herb Butter And Crispy Skin

A weeknight staple delivering 42g of protein per serving. Compound butter of thyme, rosemary, and lemon zest — applied under and over the skin for maximum flavor.
Planned by Jen  ·  45 min  ·  4 servings  ·  Added Mar 28
Roasted Chicken Thighs

Begin by patting the chicken thighs completely dry — this is the single most important step for achieving crispy skin. Combine softened butter with finely minced thyme, rosemary, lemon zest, and a generous amount of cracked black pepper. Work half the butter under the skin directly onto the meat, and rub the remainder over the exterior.
