Almond Croissant Bars — Optimization Analysis

Current per serving: 604 kcal · 31g fat · 5.3g sat fat · 62g carbs · 13g sugar · 20.6g protein · 13g fiber

The bones of this recipe are genuinely excellent — the protein and fiber are remarkable for a snack bar. The areas to work on are calories (604 is high for a snack), fat (mainly from butter and almonds), and sugar (double hit of maple syrup in both layers).

Proposed Changes

  • Replace butter (4 tbsp) → 2 tbsp butter + 2 tbsp applesauce — cuts ~200 kcal, ~16g fat across the batch
  • Reduce base maple syrup ¼ cup → 3 tbsp — saves ~54 kcal, ~12g sugar
  • Reduce topping maple syrup 3 tbsp → 2 tbsp — saves ~55 kcal, ~12g sugar
  • Reduce slivered almonds ¾ cup → ½ cup — saves ~37 kcal/serving, ~8g fat
  • Skip powdered sugar dusting — swap for toasted almond flour or skip
