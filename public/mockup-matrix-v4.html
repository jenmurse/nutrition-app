Your other questions answered
What did I mean by "Recent" tab?

A picker tab showing recipes you've added to plans in the last ~30 days. Useful for "I cooked that two weeks ago, it worked, let me put it in again." Requires tracking that we may or may not already do via MealLog timestamps. Defer to Phase 2 — Favorites + All covers the immediate need.

"+ New Plan" / duplicate from old plan

Works the same. Matrix is just a different rendering of MealLog data. Duplicate-plan copies the MealLogs from the source week. The matrix renders them as filled cells. Same model, no migration.

The "Duplicate from…" affordance in New Plan creation stays intact. Possibly even more useful in matrix because you can immediately see what got copied and swap a few cells before saving.

Background color

Fixed throughout. Mockups now use --bg: #FFFFFF, with --bg-2: #F5F5F4 for raised surfaces and --bg-3: #ECECEA for hover/tags. Matches the real app.
