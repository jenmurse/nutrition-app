Recipe Index / No. 002  ·  Protein
Cedar Plank Salmon
25 Min Prep · Anti-Inflammatory · High Omega-3
Complexity moderate / architectural
Ingredients & USDA Profiling
Wild Alaskan King Salmon #173690 180g / 36g P / 22g F
Organic Meyer Lemon 2 Slices / 1g C
Fresh Sprig Rosemary #170944 2g / 0g Macro
Himalayan Sea Salt Pinch / trace
Cold Pressed Olive Oil 15ml / 14g F
Black Garlic Cloves 2 units / 4g C
Preparation Sequence
01
Submerge the cedar
Soak your untreated cedar plank in filtered water for at least 60 minutes. This prevents combustion and ensures the essential oils of the wood permeate the tissue of the salmon.
02
Surface preparation
Pat the wild salmon dry. Apply a thin veil of cold-pressed olive oil. This acts as a conductor for heat and a carrier for the rosemary and black garlic infusion.
03
Thermal equilibrium
Preheat your oven or grill to 200°C. Place the salmon onto the damp wood. Arrange lemon slices and rosemary sprigs along the spine of the fillet.
04
The infusion
Roast for 12–15 minutes. The goal is an internal temperature of 52°C for medium-rare, preserving the delicate omega-3 fatty acids from oxidation.
