Answers to your feedback

Q · Don't love the MAY/JUN overlap

Fixed. Month labels now sit in a band above the day cells, with a dashed underline marking the cell boundaries. No more stickers on top of cells, no overlap.

Q · Is "click any day → jump matrix to that week" what you meant by Q&A?

Yes. The Q&A block in the previous mock was just a section title I used for "questions you raised + my answers." The click behavior itself was the substantive thing: click any day in the strip and the matrix navigates to the plan covering that week. No surprise plan creation — days that have no plan are dim/unclickable.

Q · Rename FILLED / EMPTY and use planner colors

Done. Legend now reads PLANNED / NOT PLANNED / THIS WEEK / TODAY. This week uses the same soft green as the today-column in the matrix (--accent-l). Today uses the same orange as the today date number (--err) — shown as an example number "5" in the legend rather than a swatch, matching how it actually appears.

Q · Should this be optional like Hide Nutrition?

Yes. Best move is to consolidate both toggles into a single VIEW ▾ menu in the top toolbar (replacing the standalone HIDE NUTRITION button). Cleaner toolbar, and we get a natural home for future view options.

On mobile, I'd add a compact VIEW button to the second toolbar (between the arrows and the ⋯). The ⋯ stays day-scoped (templates), and view toggles live in their own small sheet. This avoids overloading ⋯ with mixed concerns.

Decided · Defaults

Both toggles default ON, persist per-device in localStorage (same pattern as the current HIDE NUTRITION).
