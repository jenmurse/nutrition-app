Sheet Pan Salmon
with Roasted Vegetables
780
kcal
58g
Protein
38g
Fat
42g
Carbs
Ingredients
Salmon fillet200g
Broccoli, chopped1½ cup
Cherry tomatoes1 cup
Olive oil2 tbsp
Garlic cloves, minced4
Lemon, sliced½
Salt + pepperto taste
Instructions
01Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a large sheet pan with parchment.
02Toss vegetables in olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread on pan. Roast 15 minutes.
03Push vegetables to sides. Place salmon in center. Top with lemon slices.
04Roast another 12–15 minutes until salmon flakes easily. Season and serve.
