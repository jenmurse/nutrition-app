Overnight oats
2 servings · Prep 5 min · Cook 0 min
Breakfast
Instructions
1. In a jar, combine oats, chia seeds, and oat milk. Stir well.
2. Add almond butter and maple syrup. Mix until combined.
3. Cover and refrigerate overnight (at least 4 hours).
4. Top with sliced banana before serving. Enjoy cold.
