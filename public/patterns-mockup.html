Good Measure
Dashboard Planner Recipes
App content — the notification slides in below the nav, pushes content down slightly (no z-index overlap), then slides back up on dismiss or after 4 seconds.

On mobile, this avoids the bottom-of-screen hazard zone (notch, gesture bar, home indicator).
Behaviour rules
Success — auto-dismiss after 4s. No action needed.
Error — never auto-dismiss. Stays until user acts or retries.
Warning — auto-dismiss after 8s, or on action click.
Neutral/loading — stays until the operation resolves, then swaps to success or error.
Stacking — only one bar at a time. New notification replaces current (crossfade).
