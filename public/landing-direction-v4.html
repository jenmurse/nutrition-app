Good Measure
Software for home kitchens
Private beta · 2026

A nutrition app for people who actually cook.

Good Measure is a studio-built software for home kitchens. Every ingredient you own, every recipe you use, every meal for everyone in the house — measured to the gram, calculated live, and optimized by an AI that reads the page with you.

Get Started ↗

Most nutrition apps sell you a diet. We built a food system — the pantry, the recipes, the plan, the reasoning.

Four partsPantry, recipes, planner, AI. One data layer that flows between them so nothing is entered twice.
The differenceThe app is the measurement. The reasoning — what to swap, when to prep, why it hits your targets — is Claude, reading your recipes through MCP.
01 — Pantry
The foundation

Every ingredient, stored exactly how you use it.

USDA-backed nutrition for every ingredient in your kitchen. Store them the way you actually measure — by gram, tablespoon, serving, or custom unit — and every recipe inherits it automatically. Build once. Cook forever.
