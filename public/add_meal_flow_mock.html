Transitions between steps and pages
Documenting the timing values for the editorial transitions across Add Meal, Shopping list, Compare, and any future full-screen page. Keeps motion legible across the system.
Page enters from right
Add Meal Step 1 opening, Shopping opening, Compare loading. Underlying page stays. New page slides in 24px from the right while fading from 0 to 1.
400ms · ease-out
Step-to-step within flow
Add Meal Step 1 → Step 2. Step 1 fades out and slides 16px left. Step 2 fades in and slides 16px from right. Crossfade overlap.
320ms · ease-in-out
Page exits
Hitting BACK from any editorial page. Reverse of entry — slides right 24px while fading to 0.
280ms · ease-in
Confirmation modals
Delete confirmations and similar small dialogs. Fade in with 8px upward translate. Background dim fades in parallel.
200ms · ease-out

Grid/List toggle, sort change, filter chip selection. Opacity-only crossfade. Fast enough to feel instant but smooth enough to avoid jank.
