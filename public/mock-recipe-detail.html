Prep notes

Heat oil in a large wok or pan over medium-high heat. Add curry paste and fry for 2 minutes until fragrant. Add chicken and cook until sealed. Pour in coconut milk and add lime leaves. Simmer 15 minutes. Add eggplant and fish sauce. Cook a further 5 minutes. Serve over steamed jasmine rice.
