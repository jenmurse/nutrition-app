```
At a Glance / Pantry / Recipes / Optimize / Planner / Everyone
```

**`FEATURES`** — the number, bold title, and supporting sentence shown in the description bar when each tab is active:
```
01  At a glance          Today's nutrition, meals, and this week's plan — all on one screen.
02  Build your pantry    USDA lookup or manual entry. Your ingredient library, your way.
03  Import or create recipes   Paste a URL, upload a file, or build from scratch. Nutrition calculates live.
04  Optimize with AI     Claude reads the recipe, suggests swaps, and saves notes back automatically.
05  Plan your week       Weekly grid per person. Add meals, see daily totals, hit your targets.
06  Track the household  Each person's plan, side by side. One recipe library shared by everyone.
```
